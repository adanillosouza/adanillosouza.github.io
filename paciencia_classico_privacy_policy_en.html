Privacy Policy - Classic Solitaire

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Summary: Classic Solitaire respects your privacy. This game collects minimal data necessary for basic functionality and advertising. We do not sell your personal data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Classic Solitaire ("we", "our" or "Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you play our classic solitaire game.

By using Classic Solitaire, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Game Data

  • Game Progress: Scores, win/loss statistics, number of completed games, personal records, game time
  • Settings: Sound preferences, visual themes, selected game mode
  • Locally stored data: All game progress is stored locally on your device

2.2. Device Information

  • Device model, Android operating system version
  • Google advertising identifier (GAID)
  • App crash and performance information
  • IP address and connection information

2.3. Analytics Data

  • Game time, sessions started
  • Interactions with game features
  • App performance data
  • Anonymous usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Game Functionality: Save your progress, maintain statistics and personal records
  • Improvements: Analyze usage patterns to improve gameplay and fix bugs
  • Advertising: Display relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud or malicious activities
  • Support: Respond to technical support requests

4. Third-Party Services

Classic Solitaire integrates the following third-party services:

4.1. Google AdMob

  • Displays ads within the game
  • Collects advertising identifiers and ad interaction data
  • May use cookies and similar technologies for personalized advertising

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Manage ad preferences: https://adssettings.google.com

4.2. Google Play Services

  • In-app purchase management (if applicable)
  • Analytics and statistics services

4.3. Firebase Analytics (Google)

  • Collects anonymous usage data for performance analysis
  • Information about app behavior

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal data.

We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With Google AdMob and Firebase for ad functionality and analytics
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Rights Protection: To protect our rights, property, or safety

6. Data Storage

  • Local Data: Your game progress and settings are stored locally on your device
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous data collected by Firebase and AdMob is retained according to those platforms' policies
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the game removes all local data. Anonymous analytics data may remain in third-party services

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure storage of local data on device
  • Encrypted communication with third-party servers (HTTPS)
  • Use of anonymous identifiers when possible

Important: No storage or transmission method is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1. Ad Control

  • Disable personalized ads in Google ad settings
  • Use your Android device's privacy settings to limit tracking

8.2. Data Deletion

  • Game Data: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Analytics Data: To request deletion of analytics data, please contact us

8.3. App Permissions

Classic Solitaire requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your progress and settings
  • Internet: To display ads and collect anonymous analytics
  • Network State Access: To check connectivity before loading ads

9. Children's Privacy

Classic Solitaire is suitable for all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove that information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country, including in the United States (where Google/Firebase maintain servers).

By using Classic Solitaire, you consent to this transfer. We use providers that adhere to international data protection standards.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Displaying a notification in the game (for important changes)

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.

12. GDPR Compliance - European Union

For users in the European Union, we ensure your rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
