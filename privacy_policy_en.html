Privacy Policy - Michely App

Last Updated: October 4, 2025

Summary: Michely App respects your privacy. We collect only the information necessary to provide our language practice services and protect your data with security.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Michely App ("we", "our" or "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our artificial intelligence language practice mobile application.

By using Michely App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Account Information

  • Google Authentication: Name, email address, profile picture, and unique ID when you sign in with your Google account
  • User ID: Unique identifier generated by Firebase Authentication

2.2. App Usage Data

  • Learning Progress: Number of conversations, practice time, scores, streak days
  • Settings: Native language, target language, difficulty level, voice preferences
  • Conversation History: Messages exchanged with AI (securely stored in Firebase Firestore)

2.3. Audio Data

  • Voice Recordings: When you use voice recognition, we temporarily process your audio to convert it to text. We do not permanently store your audio recordings.
  • Audio processing is done through third-party services (Google Speech-to-Text)

2.4. Device Information

  • Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App crash and performance information
  • IP address and connection information

2.5. Purchase Data

  • Subscription and in-app purchase history (processed through Google Play Billing)
  • We do not collect or store credit card information - all payment processing is done by Google Play

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide the Service: Process your conversations with AI, save your progress, personalize your learning experience
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and fix bugs
  • Communication: Send push notifications about your progress, practice reminders (with your consent)
  • Manage Subscriptions: Process and manage your PRO subscription
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, protect against malicious activities
  • Advertising: Display personalized ads (only for non-PRO users) through Google AdMob

4. Third-Party Services

Michely App integrates the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1. Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Login management
  • Cloud Firestore: User data storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications
  • Firebase Remote Config: Dynamic app configuration

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2. Google AdMob

  • Displays personalized ads for non-PRO users
  • Collects advertising identifiers and ad interaction data

Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.3. Google Speech-to-Text

  • Processes audio for voice recognition
  • Audio is temporarily processed and not permanently stored

4.4. Google Play Billing

  • Processes in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Manages payment information (we don't have access to card data)

5. Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal data.

We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With the third-party services mentioned above to operate the application
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Rights Protection: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or those of our users

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services while you have an active account
  • Comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements

If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except data we must retain for legal requirements.

7. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data encryption at rest in Firebase
  • Strict access controls to systems
  • App integrity verification with Firebase App Check

Important: No transmission or storage method is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1. Data Access and Control

  • Access: View the data we have about you
  • Correct: Update incorrect information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Receive a copy of your data in portable format

8.2. App Permissions

You can manage app permissions in your device settings:

  • Microphone: Required for voice recognition (can be disabled)
  • Notifications: For practice reminders (optional)

8.3. Ad Personalization

  • Manage ad preferences at: https://adssettings.google.com
  • Or subscribe to Michely PRO to remove ads completely

9. Children's Privacy

Michely App is not directed to children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected data from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside your country, where data protection laws may be different. By using Michely App, you consent to this transfer.

We use service providers (Firebase/Google) that adhere to internationally recognized data protection frameworks.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email or in-app notification (for important changes)

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically. Changes take effect immediately after being posted.

12. GDPR Compliance - European Users

For users in the European Union, we ensure your rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Rights related to automated decision-making
